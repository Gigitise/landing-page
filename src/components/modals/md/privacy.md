# Data Protection Declaration

This privacy notice is in place to inform how we at Gigitise (hereafter "Gigitise") collect, use, and protect personal data. The term ‘personal data’ refers to any information relating to an identified or identifiable living individual. We collect and use personal data to provide our services effectively and are committed to being transparent and respectful in handling personal data.

**Collection of Personal Data**

We may collect personal data when you,

- Create an account on our platform
- Use our services to post gigs or hire freelancers
- Participate in surveys or provide feedback
- Contact our support team
- Visit our website

The information we collect may include your name, contact details, payment information, and other relevant details necessary for the provision of our services.

**Use of Personal Data**

The personal data you provide may be used,

- To create and manage your account
- To facilitate the hiring process between clients and freelancers
- To communicate with you about your account and our services
- To provide customer support and address inquiries
- To improve our services and tailor them to your needs
- For marketing purposes, with your explicit consent

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes without your prior consent. Data may be shared with trusted partners and service providers only for the purposes outlined in this privacy policy.

**Protection of Personal Data** - We implement security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Your personal information is stored securely on our servers, and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

**Your Rights** - You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can also object to the processing of your personal data or request its transfer. To exercise these rights, please contact us at [contact email].

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies** - We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website and improve our services. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

**Data Retention** - We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy or as required by law. When personal data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

**Third-Party Links** - Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites.

**Controller/Responsibility** - Gigitise is the controller responsible for processing your data. Our contact details are provided below.

**Questions** - We may update this privacy notice periodically. If you have any questions or concerns about this notice, please contact us at **info@gigitise.com**.
